# Apple Social Media Analytics for Strategic Branding
## Project Overview
This project analyzes the digital marketing performance of Apple using Excel analytics techniques. It evaluates social media performance, campaign engagement, ad spend efficiency, and product launch impact to make data-driven strategic insights..

## Project Objective:
To build a Social Media Intelligence Solution in Excel that consolidates Apple's raw data into actionable business insights. The analysis helps to:
* Identify high-performing content
* Measure campaign ROI
* Track engagement & follower growth
* Optimize platform strategy
* Improve long-term brand advocacy

## Dataset Description
1) Posts Dataset : Post-level data across platforms
2) Engagement Summary Dataset : Weekly aggregated platform-level metrics
3) Campaign Metadata Dataset : Ad Campaigns' details

## Tools & Technology used: 
* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query Editor for Data Cleaning & Preparation
* Power Pivot
* PivotTables and PivotCharts
* ROI and Engagement Uplift Analysis

## Key Findings
### Content Performance
* Video content has the highest engagement(Avg Engagement: 0.139)
* Carousel and Image content follow closely
* Videos perform strongest across Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube
  
### Hashtag Effectiveness
* Most used hashtag: #AppleEvent (80 posts)
* Highest engagement hashtags: #HealthTech, #AppleWatchUltra2
* Lifestyle & product-focused hashtags outperform generic tags

### Platform Analysis
* YouTube records highest overall engagement
* Twitter performs strongly for announcements
* Multi-platform presence outperforms single-platform focus

### Campaign Effectiveness
* Campaigns drive follower spikes.
* Highest ROI: Sustainability Awareness Drive (2.52)
* High engagement uplift combined with controlled ad spend drives stronger ROI.
  
### Follower Growth & Retention
* Launch weeks show clear growth spikes
* Weak direct correlation between ad spend and follower gain

## Recommendations
* Prioritize video-led storytelling across platforms.
* Leverage high-performing hashtags.
* Allocate ad budget based on engagement efficiency, not spend volume.
* Maintain a multi-platform strategy with platform-specific content optimization.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how Excel-based analytics can translate social media data into actionable insights for Apple. The findings highlight that video-led content, optimized campaigns, and efficient ad spend drive stronger engagement and sustainable follower growth.
